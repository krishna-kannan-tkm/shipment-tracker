FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

You can search using your PO Number or TKM Position Number. Partial matches are supported — you don't need to type the full reference.
The tracker pulls live data directly from TKM Global's internal system every time you open or reload the page, so you always see the latest status.
LCL stands for Less than Container Load. It means your cargo doesn't fill an entire shipping container, so it is consolidated with other shippers' goods into a shared container. LCL is ideal for smaller shipments where booking a full container would not be cost-effective.
FCL (Full Container Load) means your shipment occupies one or more dedicated containers exclusively. Breakbulk (BB) refers to cargo that cannot fit into a standard container and is shipped as individual pieces — such as heavy machinery, oversized equipment, or project cargo. TKM Global handles both FCL and Breakbulk under the same service offering.
ETD (Estimated Time of Departure) is the scheduled date the vessel or aircraft is expected to leave the origin port or airport. ETA (Estimated Time of Arrival) is the scheduled date it is expected to arrive at the destination. Once the vessel/flight has actually departed or arrived, these are shown as ATD (Actual Time of Departure) and ATA (Actual Time of Arrival).
A date marked (Planned) is scheduled but has not yet occurred. Once the date has passed, it is treated as completed and shown without the label.
Each milestone represents a key stage in your shipment journey. Completed milestones are shown with a filled circle in the freight colour; upcoming steps remain grey. For Sea LCL: Order Received → Booked → Picked Up → Arrived at Warehouse → Vessel Departed → Pre-alert Sent. For Air: Order Received → Booked → Picked Up → At Airport → Flight Departed → Pre-alert Sent → Flight Arrived.
For any queries not answered by the tracker, please contact your TKM Global operations contact directly. Their contact details are included in your shipment documentation and pre-alert notification.
Who We Are

About TKM Global

TKM Global GmbH, a TATA group company, is a global supply chain and logistics service provider. Established in 1986, TKM Global has over 35 years of experience in the area of international ocean and air freight services, project cargo handling, relocation of used plant and machinery, inventory and warehouse management and supply chain services.

With a young, multi-cultural team having extensive domain expertise, TKM Global has developed itself into an integrated logistics provider with bespoke services to meet the individual demands of its customers. We continue to carry the values of the TATA Group on which rests the foundation of our business.
